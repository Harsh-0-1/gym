Timetable to follow :


  • 1) Wake up early around 5 to 6 am in morning
  • 2) Then go an freshen up
  • 3) Then do exercise until you feel sweaty
  • 4) Then take bath after half an hour has passed (recommended cold water)
  • 5) Then meditate before breakfast
  • 6) Then do breakfast
  • 7) Then do your work till 1or 2 pm
  • 8) Then do lunch (in heavy amount)
  • 9) Then work or follow your hobby
  • 10)Eat dinner from 8-9 pm
  • 11)Then sleep for 8hrs
  • 12)And the cycle continues
